Synapsis

In New York City's gritty East Village, a group of bohemians strive for success and acceptance while
enduring the obstacles of poverty, illness and the AIDS epidemic.

Storyline

This rock opera tells the story of one year in the life of a group of bohemians struggling in modern
day East Village New York. The story centers around Mark and Roger, two roommates. While a former
tragedy has made Roger numb to life, Mark tries to capture it through his attempts to make a
film. In the year that follows, the group deals with love, loss, AIDS, and modern day life in one truly
powerful story.
